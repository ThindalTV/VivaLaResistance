## Description

**Stop squinting at tiny color bands.**

Viva La Resistance uses your camera and advanced computer vision to instantly identify resistor values in real time. Just point, and the answer appears — right there, floating next to the component.

### ⚡ Why Viva La Resistance?

**Instant AR overlays** — The resistor value appears directly in your camera view, anchored next to each component. No switching screens, no typing, no charts.

**Multiple resistors, one glance** — Detect several resistors simultaneously. Sorting through a parts bin? This app handles the chaos.

**Works completely offline** — All processing happens on your device. No internet connection required. No cloud. No waiting. Ever.

**Fast and focused** — Open the app, point your camera, get answers. That's it. No accounts, no setup, no tutorials.

**Real-world lighting ready** — Built to handle the lighting conditions you actually work in, from your bench lamp to natural daylight.

### 🎯 Perfect For

• Electronics hobbyists and makers
• Engineering students
• Repair technicians
• Anyone tired of memorizing color codes

### 📱 How It Works

The app uses a trained machine learning model (YOLOv8-nano) running entirely on your device to locate resistors in your camera feed. Once detected, color band analysis identifies the resistance value and tolerance. Results display as clean AR badges positioned next to each component.

### 🔒 Privacy First

We request camera access only. No photos are saved. No data leaves your device. No internet connection needed. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts.

### 💚 Support Development

This app is made by one person. It's fully functional from day one. After 7 days, you'll see an occasional, easily-dismissed prompt asking if you'd like to support continued development. No features are ever locked. No pressure.
