## Overview

Viva La Resistance ("the App") is a mobile application that uses your device's camera to identify resistor values in real time. This privacy policy explains what data the App accesses, how it's used, and your rights.

**The short version:** We access your camera. That's it. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything happens on your device.

---

## Information We Access

### Camera Access

The App requires access to your device's camera to function. The camera feed is used to:

- Detect resistors in the live camera view
- Analyze color bands to calculate resistance values
- Display AR overlays showing the identified values

**Camera access is used only while the App is open and active.** The App does not:

- Record video
- Save photos or screenshots
- Access your photo library or camera roll
- Use the camera when the App is in the background

### No Other Permissions

The App does not request or use:

- Microphone access
- Photo library access
- Location services
- Contacts or address book
- Bluetooth or network hardware (beyond basic OS functions)
- Any other device sensors or data

## Internet Connectivity

**The App does not require internet connectivity to function.**

The App operates entirely offline. All image processing and resistor detection happens locally on your device using an embedded machine learning model. No data is transmitted over the internet.

The App does not:

- Make network requests
- Connect to any servers
- Download or upload any content
- Check for updates (updates are handled through the App Store / Google Play only)

## Your Rights

Because we don't collect personal data, traditional data subject rights (access, deletion, portability) don't apply in the usual sense. However:

- **Camera access:** You can revoke camera permission at any time through your device settings
- **App data:** Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data
- **Questions:** You may contact us with any privacy concerns

## Security

All processing occurs locally on your device within the App's sandboxed environment, protected by your device's operating system security. No data is transmitted externally, eliminating network-based security risks.
